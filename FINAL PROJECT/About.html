About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a passionate graphic designer specializing in creating visually stunning designs for Pomida's Tailoring and Prints. While my primary focus is graphic design, I also have entry-level proficiency in web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My expertise lies in Adobe's creative suite, including Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Audition. Currently, I'm honing my skills as a student at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology, where I'm immersed in the latest trends and techniques in design and technology. Whether it's crafting eye-catching visuals or delving into the world of web development, I'm dedicated to bringing creativity and innovation to every project I undertake.
